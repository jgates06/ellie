Basset Hound Round Up

Home of all things Basset!

Hello, my name is Jeff and I, along with my family, live in Lee’s Summit, MO. Through this page I hope to share the joys of living with the Basset Hound. I have had several Bassets over the years so I’d like to think I’ve gotten to know the breed fairly well by now. We lost Fred, our last Basset October 2015 after a long happy 14 ½ years. We lasted almost exactly a year of being pet free until the Basset bug caught us again. Last October we brought home 6 month old Ellie.
