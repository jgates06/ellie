Chocolate Chip Cookies

A classic chocolate chip cookie recipe that sets itself apart by incorporating oatmeal and shredded coconut for added body and texture.
  • ½ cup white granulated sugar
  • ½ cup brown granulated sugar
  • 1 stick of butter (room temperature)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ shredded coconut
  • 1 cup of quick oats
  • ½ cup of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  1. Combine sugar, butter (softened to room temperature), egg and vanilla in a large bowl.
  2. Sift flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt slowly into batter and stir until combined into a dough.
  3. Mix in coconuts, oats and chocolate chips
  4. Drop 1.5” balls onto cookie sheet leaving 2 inches of separation.
  5. Bake at 350* for 9-11 minutes or until slightly golden brown.
  6. Remove from oven and let cool on racks.
