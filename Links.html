Basset Hound Round Up

Ready to get slobbered?

So you have decided you are ready for the easy going Basset life style. I personally recommend adopting a dog from a rescue or a local shelter before looking at the breeders. All of my Bassets have come from shelters or adopted from a previous owner and they all have been really good dogs. I have included some links for some local (to me) basset rescue or local shelters that would be great places to begin your search.

  1. Western Missouri Basset Rescue, Inc

  2. The Humane Society of Greater Kansas City

  3. The Humane Society of Greater Kansas City

  4. Basset and Beagle Rescue of the Heartland (BBRH)

  5. Wayside Waifs
