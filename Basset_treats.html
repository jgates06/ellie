Basset Hound Round Up

Dog Treats you can make yourself!

Peanut Butter Dog Biscuits

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups whole wheat flour
  2. 1 tablespoon baking powder
  3. 1 cup peanut butter
  4. 1 cup milk
  5. 3 tablespoons coconut oil

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375F.
  2. Combine all ingredients into mixing bowl.
  3. Using an electric mixer, mix on medium speed until blended.
  4. Place dough on a floured surface and kneed.
  5. Roll out the dough 1/2 inch thick.
  6. Cut out the shapes and place on a greased cookie sheet.
  7. Bake for 18 minutes.
  8. Allow 10 mins to cool and serve to your favorite pooch.

Pumpkin Dog Biscuits

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cups pumpkin
  2. 2 eggs
  3. 3 tablespoons peanut butter
  4. 1 1/2 - 4 cups Oat flour or wheat flour
  5. 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Whisk together pumpkin, eggs and peanut butter.
  3. Fold in 2 1/2 cups flour and the cinnamon. Add additional flour until the dough is no longer sticky.
  4. Roll dough 1/4 - 1/2 inch thickness and cut with desired cookie cutters.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes or until crisp.
  6. Allow 10 mins to cool and serve to your favorite pooch.
