Basset Hound Round Up

Is a Basset Hound right for you?

Main Traits:

  1. Loyal
  2. Very personable
  3. Moderate maintenance
  4. Generally low barkers
  5. Very personable
  6. Moderate maintenance
  7. Can howl (especially with storm sirens)
  8. Large Lap Dogs
  9. Gentle with children
  10. Bred to hunt small game such as rabbits, so will chase
  11. Very smell driven (long ears will hold scent closer to nose)
  12. None of mine have been diggers but some can be
  13. None of mine have been diggers but some can be
  14. Has the classic hound coat that gets oily, will need a bath semi-regular basis
  15. Moderate shedding
  16. Can howl (especially with storm sirens)
  17. Will need ears cleaned semi-regular (with baths)
  18. Keep toe nails trimmed
  19. Weighs from 40 lbs (female) up to 80 lbs (male). Mine have always been around 50-55lbs
  20. Bassets are really smart, but are very stubborn
  21. Trainable if you start young or work with them a lot
